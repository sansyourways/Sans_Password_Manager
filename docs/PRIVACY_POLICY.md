# Privacy Policy for Sans Password Manager (SPM)
Effective Date: January 2025  
© 2025 Sansyourways. All Rights Reserved.

Sans Password Manager (“SPM”, “the Software”, “we”) is designed to maximize your privacy.  
SPM is a **local-only**, **offline-first**, **client-side encrypted** password manager.  
We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

By using SPM, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Do NOT Collect
SPM does **not** collect, store, process, transmit, or share any of the following:

- Passwords or secure notes  
- Vault contents or metadata  
- Master password  
- Recovery keys or private keys  
- Usage analytics or behavior data  
- Device identifiers or hardware information  
- IP addresses or network logs  
- Contact details or personal identity  
- Payment information  
- Crash logs, diagnostics, or telemetry  

SPM contains **no servers**, **no cloud sync**, **no tracking**, **no remote storage**.

All encrypted vault data remains **exclusively on your device**, under your control.

---

## 2. Data You Store in SPM
All data you create inside SPM is stored locally on your device, including:

- Password entries  
- Secure notes  
- Vault files (`*.gpg`)  
- Portable bundles  
- Backup ZIPs  

These files are **encrypted with GnuPG** or **AES-based encryption**, depending on your configuration.

SPM has **no ability** to access or recover your data.

---

## 3. Master Password & Encryption Keys
Your master password:

- Never leaves your device  
- Never sent over any network  
- Never stored in plaintext  
- Cannot be recovered by the developer

If you lose your master password or key, the developer cannot help restore your vault.  
You alone are responsible for backup and key management.

---

## 4. Local Storage Only
SPM does not interact with any external or remote servers.

All operations occur locally, including:

- Encryption  
- Decryption  
- Vault management  
- Portable bundle creation  
- Web mode (if enabled, runs locally on device only)

Web Mode hosts on a **private local loopback address** and never transmits data to the internet.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services
SPM does not integrate or rely on third-party:

- Cloud services  
- Analytics providers  
- Advertising networks  
- Data processors  
- Telemetry frameworks  
- SDK tracking tools  

No third-party receives any data from SPM.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy
SPM does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

---

## 7. Paid Versions or Commercial Use
If you obtain a commercial or private-build license, SPM remains fully offline.  
No remote verification, telemetry, or usage tracking is used.
